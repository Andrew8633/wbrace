What is a Board Race?

A board race is a high-energy classroom activity where students compete in teams to fill in answers on the board. It’s ideal for practicing vocabulary, grammar, and quick thinking — especially in ESL classrooms.

How to Use WB Race

  • Create or load a template with up to 10 prompts or words.
  • Load the template to the race screen to project in class.
  • Students compete to write the answers quickly on the board.
  • You can edit, rename, and reuse any set anytime — from the same computer.

Note: All data is saved locally in your browser. Your sets won’t carry over between devices.
